CAR DRIVING SIMULATOR

PasaHero Car Driving Simulator is a brand new training tool for both beginner and experienced drivers to practice real world situations. It is a real car like replication with the controls of a actual car like a steering wheel, gears, brakes, pedals etc. The simulator hardware and software are fully customizable for the spectrum of vehicles from sedans to SUVs, 4x4s, vans and it can be configured for the vehicles running on both left handed and right handed roads. It has been used by driving schools and vocational training centers, automotive research facilities, logistics, and transportation companies.
